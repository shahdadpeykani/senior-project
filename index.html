An AI-Powered Multi-Agent Intelligence and Venture Planning Platform

Stratify is an AI-powered multi-agent business intelligence and venture planning platform designed to help aspiring entrepreneurs transform ideas into structured, data-driven business plans. By combining Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), intelligent AI agents, market analysis, financial modeling, and risk assessment, Stratify guides users from initial idea validation to execution strategy. The platform functions as an AI-powered business co-founder, generating comprehensive feasibility reports, structured financial projections, and even a starter website, making startup planning more accessible, analytical, and execution-oriented.
